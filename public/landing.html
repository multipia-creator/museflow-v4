About Us

Building the Future of Museums

Our Mission

MuseFlow.life was created to revolutionize how museums manage their operations. We believe that cultural institutions deserve world-class technology that enables them to focus on what matters most: preserving and sharing culture.

Why MuseFlow?

Traditional museum workflow management is complex, time-consuming, and error-prone. MuseFlow leverages cutting-edge AI technology to automate routine tasks, provide intelligent insights, and enable seamless collaboration.

Our multi-agent system orchestrates 8 specialized AI agents that work together to handle everything from exhibition planning to visitor analytics, all powered by Google's Gemini 2.0 and running on Cloudflare's global edge network.

Gemini 2.0 Flash TypeScript 5.7 Hono 4.10 Cloudflare Workers D1 Database Durable Objects Three.js WebXR API Chart.js Notion API
